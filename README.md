**On darkness:** The pineal gland is most active in complete darkness. Conduct evening meditation in total darkness. Ancient mystery schools used underground chambers for this reason.

## IV. Discipline Four — Dissolve the Ego

> *"Unless a grain of wheat falls into the earth and dies, it remains alone."* — John 12:24

Ego identification is, physiologically, a chronic low-grade stress response — activating the sympathetic nervous system continuously, degrading the spinal channel with stress hormones. The descent to parasympathetic — from defence to receptivity — is not merely desirable. It *is* the process. The ancients called this surrender. It is simultaneously psychological and neurological.

**Practice — Witness Consciousness (*sakshi bhava*):** When you notice identification with a thought, simply name it: "Thinking." "Defending." "Wanting." Do not suppress or follow. Recognise it as weather in the field of awareness and return to the breath. Ask: *"Am I aware of the present moment?"* The act of asking shifts the centre of gravity from ego to witness instantaneously. Repeat until stable.

## V. Discipline Five — Fast

> *"And Jesus, when he had fasted forty days and forty nights..."* — Matthew 4:2

Every initiatic tradition uses fasting: Jesus (40 days), Moses (40 days), Muhammad (Ramadan), Buddha (extended ascetic periods), the Eleusinian mysteries (several days pre-initiation). This is not coincidence.

**The neurophysiology:** After 16–24 hours without food, the brain shifts to ketone metabolism — cleaner, more efficient. Fasting elevates BDNF (neural plasticity) and dramatically amplifies sensitivity of the brain's tryptaminergic systems — the systems through which the pineal gland's DMT production is regulated. The fasting practitioner's pineal gland is operating in a body whose sensitivity to its own endogenous tryptamines has been significantly amplified.

**Protocol:**
- **Daily:** 16:8 intermittent fasting (8-hour eating window)
- **Monthly:** Full fast during the 2.5-day Gethsemane window
- **Seasonally:** 3–7 day extended fast twice yearly

## VI. Discipline Six — Align Light and Darkness

> *"If therefore thine eye be single, thy whole body shall be full of light."* — Matthew 6:22

The "single eye" — the pineal gland — is governed by light and darkness via the retinohypothalamic tract. Artificial blue-spectrum light (phones, screens, LEDs) directly suppresses melatonin by signalling to the brain that it is still daytime. A practitioner using a phone until midnight is actively suppressing their pineal gland's peak production window.

**Protocol:**
- **Morning:** 15–30 minutes of direct outdoor sunlight (no sunglasses) within the first hour of sunrise. This entrains the pineal's entire production cycle for the following 24 hours
- **Evening:** Eliminate or significantly reduce artificial light after sunset. Candlelight or amber-spectrum lighting only
- **Night:** Sleep in complete darkness — no standby LEDs, no phone screens, no streetlight. Conduct evening meditation in total darkness

Within 2–3 weeks most practitioners report markedly enhanced dream intensity, hypnagogic imagery, and meditative depth — all signatures of elevated pineal activity.
